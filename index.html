St. Mary's Indian Orthodox Congregation, Santa Cruz

St.Mary's Orthodox Congregation, Santa Cruz is a congregation under The Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church's Diocese of South-West America. The congregation has been setup for the faithful in the areas in and around Santa Cruz, California - Santa Cruz, Watsonville, Los Gatos, Morgan Hill, Gilroy ..etc.


Service Schedule

Once a month (3rd Saturday) at 9:30 AM

Namaskaram starts at 9:30 AM, Holy Qurbana around 10:15 AM.


Location

219 Bean Avenue, Los Gatos, CA (St. Mary of the Immaculate Conception)
